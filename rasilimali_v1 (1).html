Why Rasilimali exists
The National Treasury is building an internal asset balance sheet (accrual accounting transition, 2024–2027, KSh 3.1B World Bank/IMF project). That work is internal. Rasilimali is the citizen layer above it — making the same data searchable, cross-referenced, and publicly accountable. The government is building the ledger. We are making the ledger visible.

Report a missing or misused asset
If you know of a government asset that should be on the register but isn't — or an asset being misused — report it here. Reports are reviewed by the Rasilimali team and, where patterns emerge, referred to the AG's VfM audit queue.
